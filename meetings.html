Meetings & location

Saturday mornings at the Los Gatos Library.

We meet weekly during the school year in the library's Teen Section. Your first visit is free and casual. Come see what a math circle feels like.

Where we meet

Los Gatos Library, Teen Section.

We gather in the Teen Section on the lower level. Look for the Math Circle sign by the entrance; a mentor will greet first-time visitors and point you to the right group.

  • Address100 Villa Avenue, Los Gatos, CA 95030
  • RoomTeen Section, lower level
  • Day & timeSaturdays, 10:00 AM – 12:00 PM
  • SeasonDuring the school year, September–May
  • ParkingFree lot on Villa Avenue and nearby street parking
  • CostFree, donations welcome, never required

A typical Saturday

How a session runs.

Two hours of warm-ups, group problem solving, and student-led share-outs. Groups run in parallel, so everyone works at the right level.

TimeActivityWhat happens
10:00 – 10:15Warm-upA short, playful puzzle to get everyone talking.
10:15 – 11:15Problem setSmall groups work a themed set: combinatorics, geometry, number theory.
11:15 – 11:45Share-outStudents present solutions and compare approaches at the board.
11:45 – 12:00To take homeOne harder problem to think about during the week.
Find your level

Three groups, running in parallel.

Grades 6–8

Explorers

New to problem solving? Start here. We build intuition with puzzles, patterns, and games. No contest experience needed.

Grades 8–10

Challengers

For students ready for AMC-style problems and their first proofs. We bridge classroom math and competition thinking.

Grades 10–12

Olympians

Advanced problem solving and team preparation for the AIME and ARML. Bring your hardest questions.

Plan your first visit

Just show up, or say hello first.

Visiting is free and easy. Drop in to any Saturday session, or send a short note so we can save you a seat and place you in the right group.

  • BringA pencil, scratch paper, and curiosity.
  • No prepYou don't need to be a competition star, just willing to be stuck for a while.
  • Any weekDrop in whenever you can. There's no attendance requirement.
