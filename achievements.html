Highlight

HiMCM 2025: Finalist

Our modeling team earned a Finalist designation, the highest award category, on Problem B of the 2025 High School Mathematical Contest in Modeling, competing as Team #17561. Over a long November weekend they built and defended a model of a real-world problem, then wrote it up as a full paper. Read the story in the newsletter.
